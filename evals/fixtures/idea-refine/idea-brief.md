# Initial idea

“Some kind of tool that helps teams share knowledge better.”

What is known:

- The first users would be product and engineering teams of 10–50 people.
- They already use chat, issue tracking, and a shared document workspace.
- Important decisions are often difficult to find three months later.

What is not known:

- Whether the core problem is capture, discovery, trust, or maintenance.
- Who owns keeping knowledge current.
- Whether another destination or another integration would reduce friction.
- What behavior would prove the tool is useful.
